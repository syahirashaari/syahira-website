MY EXPERIENCE


My first work experience After finishing STPM and relaxing with my friends for about 3 weeks, I thought about what else to do. Still, it turns out that my educational assignment ended too early.

"I also know that you like playing with young nieces, nephews and cousins, so come here and tell me until I find a permanent teacher." My cousin Aisha called me and offered me a job. So I got my first job as a temporary teacher at the Tutor Centre. Sometimes I felt like Jill fall trades teaching Malay, English, and Sains education in third grade.

For example, I learned to study hard when I really care about my effectiveness as a teacher and whether my students are studying. Playing with a few young relatives is also quite different from dealing with 18 uncontrollable children with different learning abilities and interests.

In 4 weeks, I learned how difficult it is to be a teacher. There have been many great times to discover things together, but there have also been times when I wanted to give up, especially when some of the kids had a hard time or refused to do their homework.
